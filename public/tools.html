┗  ROTAGO Simulator

ROTAGO is a board game I worked on for a group project in my Game Design 1 class over the course of 3 weeks.

For this project, our goal was to draft a sequential, perfect information, analog game (Chess, Checkers, Go, Reversi, Connect 4, etc.) to be playtested by other groups in our class in order to make revisions for a final game. Click on the name above to watch a simulation or even play the game yourself!
